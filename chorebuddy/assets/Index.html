FAQ

Who gets to delegate the tasks?

ChoreBuddy intelligently assigns tasks to users based mostly on their preferred days. The manager can only add users and monitor completion of tasks. Each member receives a schedule which fits their prefered days.

How will I be reminded that I have a task to complete?

The manager will have inputted your phone number on the sign up form, you will receive a text message 20 minutes before you have a task reminding you of what needs to be done.

How do I let people know that I finished my task?

In your text message, you should receive a link where you can confirm that you've completed a task, send this with a picture (e.g. dishes washed) to let your manager know you actually finished your assigned task!
